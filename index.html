Hawaiian Pizza Sliders Recipe

Ingredients

  • 12 Hawaiian bread rolls
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1/4 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 6 slices deli-cut ham
  • 6 slices mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup fresh pineapple chunks
  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350째F (175째C).
  2. Slice rolls horizontally and place bottoms on a baking sheet.
  3. Spread pizza sauce and sprinkle with Italian seasoning.
  4. Layer ham, mozzarella, and pineapple chunks.
  5. Top with roll tops and brush with melted butter.
  6. Bake for 15 minutes or until rolls are golden brown.
