Hawaiian Pizza Sliders Recipe

Ingredients

  • 12 Hawaiian bread rolls
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 6 slices deli-cut ham
  • 6 slices deli-cut mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup fresh pineapple chunks
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • Pinch of salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Slice the rolls in half horizontally and place the bottoms on a baking sheet.
  3. Spread pizza sauce over the bottom halves and sprinkle with Italian seasoning.
  4. Layer ham slices, mozzarella cheese, and pineapple chunks evenly.
  5. Place the tops of the rolls back on and brush with melted butter.
  6. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, then bake for 15 minutes or until the cheese is melted and the rolls are golden brown.
  7. Let cool slightly, cut into individual sliders, and serve warm.
